About Us

Project_RC is a niche, fashion e-boutique for digital-savvy, young women who want to update their wardrobes with fresh ways to express their creative identities. The first launch will specialize in a collection of novelty, statement accessories.

We believe that young women should be inspired by and have access to unique fashion items to confidently express their creative identities. Through Project_RC’s ability to create inspiring digital content and offer quality products at affordable prices, young women can discover what style truly means.

About Project_RC
=======

Customer-oriented Service

Young women who are digital-savvy shoppers and both a follower and arbiter of fashion on social media, particularly Instagram. A small, early fanbase consisting of global customers primarily from Canada, United States, Australia, and Europe.

Project_RC is a niche fashion identity driven by styling and content creation / curation – a creative know-how which cannot be copied easily by competitors. The way Project_RC will communicate with audiences, specifically through social media, takes a hybrid influencer-retail strategy that is different from typical retail social media. This means that the content Project_RC promotes is not strictly limited to our business. Instead, the primary goal of our communication strategy is to share content that makes an emotional impact on our target audience. The goal is to show how style is a holistic expression of who you are as a person, not just the items you wear.
